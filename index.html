How to Use

  • Main Countdown: Set any future date and time at the top of the screen to start the countdown.
  • To-Do List (Left):
    • Click '+' to add a new to-do.
    • Click the circle to mark a to-do as complete.
    • Swipe left to delete a to-do.
  • Custom Counters (Right):
    • Click a counter to increment its value by 1.
    • Swipe left to decrement a counter by 1.
    • Click the '⟳' icon to reset a counter.
    • Click the '×' icon to remove a counter.
    • Click '+' to add a new counter.

Landscape Mode Recommended

For the best experience on your iPad, please rotate your device to landscape mode.
